Looks like you have found my website-- welcome! I am Parth, an undergraduate student at Gonville and Caius College , University of Cambridge . My mathematical interests range between functional analysis, algebraic geometry, and foundations. I document my thoughts and explorations in the form of articles here, or as bite-sized gists here. Hope something catches your interest!
